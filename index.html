About Us

At Jeno Energy, we believe energy = opportunity.

Every watt we deliver unlocks productivity, resilience, and long-term energy independence.

Jeno Energy is a Botswana-registered solar co-developer focused on Southern Africa.

We originate and co-develop bankable solar power projects in close collaboration with energy users, landowners, international financiers, and technical partners, taking projects from early development through financial close and delivery.

Our approach is hands-on and execution-led. We turn local solar opportunities into investment-ready assets and enable businesses, institutions, and landowners to access reliable, independent solar power without upfront capital expenditure or delivery complexity.
